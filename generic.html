Music Always Sucked

In this post I look back at horrible music from past decades.

Music today sucks, right? Vapid, soulless, recycled garbage has replaced yesteryear’s inventive genius and adventurous brilliance. These sentiments can be seen on a Youtube music video, commenters bemoaning music’s current state. Older commenters speak wistfully about back in their day, younger commentators complain they were born in the wrong decade. Bad music, bringing generations together.
